Welcome to Exercise Journal


This app will allow you to log all of you daily exercises. This way you can track your progress in an organized and efficient manner to maximize your workouts.


By using this application, you will be able to monitor the following:

  • Date
  • Exercise/Cardio
  • Weight/Time
  • Sets
  • Repetitions
